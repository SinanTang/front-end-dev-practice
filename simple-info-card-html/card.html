Satomi Ishihara

Satomi Ishihara (石原 さとみ Ishihara Satomi, born December 24, 1986 in Tokyo), also known as Kuniko Ishigami, is a Japanese actress

  • Japanese Name: 石原 さとみ
  • Age: 31
  • Top Drama: 失恋ショコラティエ
  • Top Film: わたしのグランパ

Ishihara Satomi began acting professionally in 2003 at age 17. She has won several awards such as Best Actress at both the 27th Japan Academy Prize and 46th Blue Ribbon Awards for My Grandpa.
